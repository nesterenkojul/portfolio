About

Hi! I'm Julia Nesterenko and I visualize data.

My work is focused on creating data visualizations on a broad range of topics. I mainly use Figma, Flourish, Canva and Tableau as viz tools and Python for scraping, parsing, cleaning, and transforming data.

Outside of design, I also specialize in data analysis: collecting data, transforming it, looking for patterns, testing hypotheses, answering business questions.

Graduated with excellence from the Intelligent Systems in Humanities (aka Digital Humanities) bachelor's program at ITMO University. Wrote my thesis on developing a universal tool for interactive visualisation of digital archives.

Gained some experience through: - Conducting research at the university's Digital Humanities Center.
- Working as a Laboratory Assistant in ITMO's Laboratory of Intelligent Systems and Applications.
- Working as a mentor of Data Visualization course at ITMO.
- Volunteering at the National Data Analytics Hackathon DANO as a team mentor.
- Successfully completing Consumer Insights Data Analytics Externship at Beats by Dr. Dre.

Additional activities:
- Volunteering for the better of civic community at The Sakharov Center Volunteer Center.
- Writing articles about digital technologies and society for the "Системный Блокъ" magazine.
- Novaya Gazeta Europe's Investigative and Data Journalism Summer School (2023).

I adore cooking and recipe development so much so I even expose it for public scrutiny on my petite foodblog. I find a lot of joy in bringing people together for occasional dinner clubs and exploring fascinating stories from the field of food anthropology. Worked as a food writer and social media manager for Saint Petersburg's most influential city media Sobaka.ru magazine.
From time to time I attend a movie club where we watch and discuss movies with my fellow cinemaholics.
Spent 10 years in competitive swimming – so don't be afraid of my wardrobe-like shoulders :)

Contact me via email nesterenkojula@gmail.com or Telegram @nesterenkojul.
